The right of authorship emerges the moment the artwork is created — and cannot be withdrawn (not like intellectial property.) In order to prove the authorship, the author needs to prove the moment of creation of the artwork and its form. SharpShark helps to incorporate the date and form into blockchain and IPFS (Interplanetary File System) respectively for good and guarantees its immutability.
